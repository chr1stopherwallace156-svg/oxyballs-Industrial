# RAW RESEARCH ARCHIVE

Raw research material (topic-based batches of research exchanges)
archived **unchanged** during
[Revision 07 Source Ingestion](../../roadmaps/REV07_SOURCE_INGESTION.md).

## Rules

- Files here are **evidence**: immutable once committed — never edited,
  reformatted, deduplicated, or deleted (Constitution, Article I).
- One subdirectory per Revision 07 module, named after the module
  (e.g. `hv_precharge_shutdown/` for Module 06). The full mapping is in
  the [Baseline Index](../../specifications/rev07/00_BASELINE_INDEX.md).
- Batch files are named `batch_NN_<short_description>.md` in order of
  receipt.
- Raw material is **research, not doctrine**. Only content consolidated
  into `docs/specifications/rev07/` with traceability back to files here
  can become active specification.
- Corrections to raw material are made by archiving a new batch that
  supersedes the old one, never by editing the original.
