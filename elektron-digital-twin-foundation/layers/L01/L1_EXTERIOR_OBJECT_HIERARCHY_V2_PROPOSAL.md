## Layer Classification Scheme

| Layer | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `L1_VISIBLE_EXTERIOR` | Fully visible from standard eye-level views; strict aesthetic/texture criteria |
| `L1_VISIBLE_UNDERBODY` | Visible from low angles or through wheel wells; basic frame outlines and outer axle assemblies |
| `L2_STRUCTURAL` | Inner structural members and cab mounts; hidden but needed for kinematic accuracy |
| `L3_MECHANICAL` | Functional drivetrain/engine; deferred until visual layers complete |
| `DEFERRED_DETAIL` | Fine detail (fasteners, labels) modeled late |

This split prevents modeling complex interior/mechanical structure before exterior panels are finalized.
