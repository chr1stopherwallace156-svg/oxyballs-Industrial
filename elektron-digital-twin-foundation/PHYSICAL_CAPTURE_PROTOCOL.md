## 1. Metrology Preparation & Cleanliness

The vehicle must be prepared to minimize measurement contamination:
- Wash/degrease/dry as required.
- Strip rear frame rails of road grime and temporary paint overlays.
- Park on a level surface.
- Inflate tires to baseline reference pressure per vehicle specification (values require verification for the locked configuration).

> Any specific pressure or tooling values must be recorded as RESEARCH_REQUIRED until matched to OEM sources and/or physical placards.

## 2. Physical Reference Capture Checklist

- Scale & Calibration Placement: place magnetic metrology scale bars along frame rails and cab doors to calibrate photogrammetry and scans.
- Color Reference Targets: mount an X-Rite ColorChecker on tripod next to panel surfaces during photography.
- Photographic Lens Control: use a fixed prime lens for detail shots; a macro lens for fasteners and tire markings.
- Diffuse Lighting Grid: conduct photography under uniform light conditions; avoid hard reflections and overexposure.
- Capture passes:
  - Pass 1: eye-level profile circle
  - Pass 2: low-angle undercarriage
  - Pass 3: high-angle roof/upper outline

This is a capture procedure document; it does not implement software.
