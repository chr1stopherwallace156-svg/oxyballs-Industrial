# VISION.md — Why EDTS Exists

Build the world’s most accurate interactive engineering workspace where physical machines can be understood, planned, serviced, and visualized through one unified spatial interface.

Who this is for
- New collaborators and non-technical stakeholders.

What EDTS is
- A layered, gate-governed spatial reference built on verified engineering facts with explicit provenance.
- A durable foundation that informs conversion planning, fabrication, service, and customer presentation.

What EDTS is not
- Not a marketing render pipeline.
- Not a simulation that replaces measurement or testing.
- Not a dumping ground for unverified numbers.

North stars
- Accuracy, provenance, clarity, and maintainability.
- Minimal unknowns; explicit documentation when unknowns remain.
